## Getting Started

1. **Install a Web3 Wallet**
   - Download and install [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/) or another Web3 wallet
   - Create or import a wallet
   - Add the Base network to your wallet

2. **Fund Your Wallet**
   - Purchase ETH on an exchange
   - Bridge ETH to Base network
   - Ensure you have enough ETH for ticket purchases and gas fees

## Connecting Your Wallet

1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top-right corner
2. Select your preferred wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
3. Approve the connection in your wallet
4. Sign the message to verify ownership

## Browsing Events

1. **Explore Page**
   - View upcoming events
   - Filter by category, date, or price
   - Search for specific events or organizers

2. **Event Details**
   - View event description, date, time, and location
   - Check ticket availability and pricing
   - See event organizer details
   - View event on blockchain explorer

## Purchasing Tickets

1. Select an event and click "Get Tickets"
2. Choose the number of tickets
3. Review your order total
4. Click "Checkout"
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
6. Wait for confirmation (usually takes a few seconds)

Your tickets will appear in your wallet as NFTs. You can view them in your wallet or on the "My Tickets" page.

## Managing Your Tickets

1. **Viewing Tickets**
   - Go to "My Tickets" in your profile
   - View all your purchased tickets
   - Click on a ticket to see details

2. **Transferring Tickets**
   - Open the ticket details
   - Click "Transfer"
   - Enter the recipient's wallet address
   - Confirm the transaction in your wallet

3. **Selling Tickets**
   - Go to "My Tickets"
   - Click on the ticket you want to sell
   - Click "Sell"
   - Set your price in ETH
   - Confirm the transaction to list on the marketplace

## Creating Events (Organizers)

1. **Requirements**
   - Connected Web3 wallet
   - Sufficient ETH for gas fees
   - Event details ready (name, description, date, etc.)

2. **Steps**
   - Click "Create Event" in the navigation
   - Fill in event details
   - Set ticket price and supply
   - Upload event image (stored on IPFS)
   - Review and confirm the transaction

3. **Managing Your Event**
   - View ticket sales in real-time
   - Update event details (if needed)
   - Cancel the event (if necessary)
   - Distribute tickets to attendees

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is Eventura?**
A: Eventura is a decentralized event ticketing platform built on the Base blockchain, enabling secure, transparent, and fraud-proof ticket sales and transfers.

**Q: Do I need cryptocurrency to use Eventura?**
A: Yes, you'll need ETH on the Base network to purchase tickets and pay for gas fees.

### Tickets

**Q: How do I know if my ticket is valid?**
A: All tickets are NFTs on the blockchain. You can verify them on a blockchain explorer using the transaction hash or token ID.

**Q: Can I get a refund for my ticket?**
A: Refund policies are set by the event organizer. Please check the event details or contact the organizer directly.

### Technical

**Q: Which wallets are supported?**
A: We support MetaMask, WalletConnect, and other WalletConnect-compatible wallets.

**Q: What network should I be on?**
A: Eventura operates on the Base network. Make sure your wallet is connected to Base Mainnet.

### Troubleshooting

**I can't see my tickets in my wallet**
- Make sure you're connected to the Base network
- Check if the transaction was successful on a block explorer
- Try importing the ticket NFT contract address manually

**My transaction is stuck**
- Check the transaction status on a block explorer
- You may need to increase the gas price
- If stuck for too long, you might need to reset your wallet
